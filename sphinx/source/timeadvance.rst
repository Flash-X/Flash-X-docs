TimeAdvance
===========

The *TimeAdvance* unit implements methods
for advancing the solution. The current version of |flashx| has two
alternative implementations of TimeAdvance, one implements the strang
split method, and the other implements the method of lines. In
addition, at the highest level, the TimeAdvance unit 
organizes the interaction between units
